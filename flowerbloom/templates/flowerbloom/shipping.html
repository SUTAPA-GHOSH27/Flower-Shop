Shipping Methods

21/01/2024

Thank you for shopping at Flower Shop. Here, we provide you with information about our shipping methods to ensure a smooth and transparent shopping experienceWe understand that a seamless shopping experience is crucial, and we want to assure you that our commitment extends to delivering your orders with care and efficiency. Our team is dedicated to handpicking the freshest blooms, crafting exquisite arrangements, and ensuring that every aspect of your order, from creation to delivery, is executed with precision and attention to detail..

Shipping Destinations

We currently offer shipping to the following destinations:

  • Domestic Shipping (within India)
  • International Shipping

Please note that shipping availability may vary based on your location.

Shipping Providers

We partner with reputable shipping carriers to deliver your orders in a timely and secure manner. Our primary shipping providers include:

  • FedEx
  • DHL
  • Local Postal Service

Shipping Times

Domestic Shipping

  • Standard Shipping: 2 business days
  • Express Shipping: 1 business days

International Shipping

    Shipping times for international orders may vary based on the destination. During the checkout process, you will be provided with estimated delivery times based on your location.

Order Processing Time

Please allow 12 hr for order processing before your item is shipped. Order processing times may be affected during peak seasons or holidays.

Tracking Information

Once your order has been shipped, you will receive a shipping confirmation email containing tracking information. Use this information to track the status and location of your package.

Shipping Costs

Shipping costs are calculated based on the shipping method selected and the destination. You can view the shipping costs during the checkout process before completing your purchase.

Customs and Duties

For international orders, please be aware that customs duties and taxes may apply. These charges are the responsibility of the recipient and will be assessed by the customs authorities in the destination country.
