Payment Policies

21/01/2024

Thank you for choosing Flower Shop. Please read our Payment Policy carefully to understand the terms and conditions related to the payment process.

Accepted Payment Methods

We accept the following payment methods for purchases made on our website:

  • Credit Cards (Visa, MasterCard)
  • Debit Cards
  • PayPal
  • UPI

Payment Processing

Authorization Hold

When you place an order, an authorization hold may be placed on your credit or debit card to verify the availability of funds. The actual charge will only occur once the order is processed and ready for shipment.

Payment Confirmation

You will receive a payment confirmation via email once your payment has been successfully processed. This confirmation will serve as proof of payment.

Pricing and Currency

All prices on our website are listed in ₹. We reserve the right to update product prices and shipping fees at our discretion. Any changes will be communicated on the website.

Taxes

Applicable taxes will be calculated and displayed during the checkout process. Please note that tax rates may vary based on your location.

Refunds and Returns

For information on refunds and returns, please refer to our Refund and Return Policy.

Security

We prioritize the security of your payment information. Our website employs industry-standard security measures to protect your data during the payment process.
